YOUR BUSINESS, OUR PASSION

Van Hool is a family business with Belgian roots and a strong international outlook. In 75 years, we have conquered the world with a complete range of coaches and buses. Today, our group builds up to 1,400 buses and coaches and some 3,000 industrial vehicles annually, 80 per cent of which are for export. Our greatest pride? Our more than 3500 employees. They are the engine of our success and make Van Hool one of the most progressive and valued bus builders in Europe. Read more about Van Hool

VAN HOOL DELIVERS.

We’re in the midst of the delivery-season. The Van Hool TX and EX touringcars find their way to customers all over the world. Van Hool offers a complete range of touringcars meeting the specific requirements and the high standards set by these loyal customers. The TX is the result of the constant evolution of our range of coaches, in our continuing search for an even higher level of concept and construction. As always, these coaches are timeless and contemporary, with a focus on both passenger and driver, as well as the environment. The EX stands for efficient and elegant, multifunctional and flexible. The modular concept of the EX, coupled with Van Hool’s famous reliability, makes it a vehicle that can be adapted to the specific nature of any passenger transport, without compromising on quality, safety or comfort.
